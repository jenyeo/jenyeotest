GPS LED Watch

I made this GPS watch during my spare time. So I followed Adafruit’s tutorial on the FLORA GPS watch and made a few of my own modifications. I created the watch to also have a mode of a flashlight and changed some of the colors around. Though there are definitely better watches and GPS devices, this project was a fun way to experiment with the GPS module, neopixel ring, the FLORA board, and an accelerometer.
