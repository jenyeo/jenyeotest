Hi! My name is Jen Yeo and I'm a first year Master's student in Mechanical Engineering at Boston University. I recently graduated in May 2023 with my BS in Bioengineering (biomedical devices) and a minor in mechanical engineering at Northeastern University. I chose bioengineering because it's a merge betweeen technology and medicine. My passion is to create devices to help people in need around the world.

I've had three co-ops at Abiomed, the Masschussets General Hospital's Martinos Center for Biomedical Imaging and Resonetics. I have had the opportunity to work on interesting projects such as heart pumps, thrombectomy catheters, and MRI research!


During my free time, I enjoy crocheting, knitting, sewing, and traveling!
