P5.JS Coded Dim Sum Drawstring Bag

The assignment, “Textile Design Project,” was to create a code with an original pattern on it. The guidelines were very open but the requirements were to use loops, our own functions with inputs, correct code indentation, and citations for inspiration. I decided to make a pattern of types of dim sum: char siu bao, siu mai, and har gow. The other part of the “Dim Sum: Maker Style” is a laser cut takeout box with a 3D printed dumpling was part of a summer program I attended.
